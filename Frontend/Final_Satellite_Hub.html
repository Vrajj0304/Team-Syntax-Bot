Spatial Harmonization Engine

All satellite data is reprojected to a common 500m × 500m grid (EPSG:4326). Multi-resolution fusion uses bilinear interpolation for upscaling and area-weighted averaging for downscaling. Temporal alignment snaps observations to nearest 8-day composite windows.
